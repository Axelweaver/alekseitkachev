Privacy Policy

Politika privatnosti

Last updated · May 9, 2026 Poslednje ažuriranje · 9. maj 2026.

Aleksei Tkachev pr Beograd ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications and services.

01Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Account registration information (name, email address)
  • Profile information
  • Communications you send to us
  • Feedback and survey responses

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our applications, we may automatically collect:

  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • Usage data (app features used, time spent in app)
  • Log data (IP address, access times, crash reports)
  • Location data (if you grant permission)

02How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our applications
  • To personalize your experience
  • To communicate with you about updates, support, and promotional content
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our services
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security vulnerabilities
  • To comply with legal obligations

03Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (analytics, hosting, customer support)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share information

04Third-Party Services

Our applications may use third-party services that collect information, including but not limited to:

  • Google Play Services
  • Apple App Store Services
  • Analytics providers (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
  • Advertising networks (if applicable)

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

05Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

06Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

07Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your information
  • Objection: Object to processing of your information
  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us at: contact@alekseitkachev.dev

08Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

09International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

11Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

01Informacije koje prikupljamo

1.1 Informacije koje pružate

Možemo prikupljati informacije koje nam dobrovoljno pružate, uključujući:

  • Informacije o registraciji naloga (ime, email adresa)
  • Informacije o profilu
  • Komunikacije koje nam šaljete
  • Povratne informacije i odgovore na ankete

1.2 Automatski prikupljene informacije

Kada koristite naše aplikacije, možemo automatski prikupljati:

  • Informacije o uređaju (tip uređaja, operativni sistem, jedinstveni identifikatori uređaja)
  • Podatke o upotrebi (korišćene funkcije aplikacije, vreme provedeno u aplikaciji)
  • Log podatke (IP adresa, vreme pristupa, izveštaji o greškama)
  • Podatke o lokaciji (ako date dozvolu)

02Kako koristimo vaše informacije

Prikupljene informacije koristimo u sledeće svrhe:

  • Za pružanje, održavanje i poboljšanje naših aplikacija
  • Za personalizaciju vašeg iskustva
  • Za komunikaciju sa vama o ažuriranjima, podršci i promotivnom sadržaju
  • Za analizu obrazaca upotrebe i poboljšanje naših usluga
  • Za otkrivanje, sprečavanje i rešavanje tehničkih problema i sigurnosnih propusta
  • Za usklađivanje sa pravnim obavezama

03Deljenje i otkrivanje podataka

Ne prodajemo vaše lične informacije. Možemo deliti vaše informacije u sledećim okolnostima:

  • Pružaoci usluga: Sa trećim stranama koje pružaju usluge u naše ime (analitika, hosting, korisnička podrška)
  • Zakonski zahtevi: Kada to zahteva zakon ili radi zaštite naših prava i sigurnosti
  • Poslovni transferi: U vezi sa spajanjem, preuzimanjem ili prodajom imovine
  • Sa vašom saglasnošću: Kada eksplicitno pristanete na deljenje informacija

04Usluge trećih strana

Naše aplikacije mogu koristiti usluge trećih strana koje prikupljaju informacije, uključujući ali ne ograničavajući se na:
